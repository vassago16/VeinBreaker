# ‚≠ê **11.1 VEINSCORE ‚Äî THE CURRENCY OF ASCENT**

Veinscore is spent to increase Attributes and unlock higher Tiers.

- You gain Veinscore for defeating enemies.
- More Veinscore for stylish chains, close calls, executions.
- Massive Veinscore for impressing the Dungeon.

### **When you spend Veinscore, you level up.**

Level is equal to how much you have spent total, not how much you currently hold.

Unspent Veinscore is dangerous‚Äîif you die, it is lost.

# ‚≠ê **11.3 TIER SYSTEM ‚Äî EARNING STYLE, NOT GRIND**

Tier unlocks are **not automatic**. They require:

- Spending the Veinscore threshold **and**
- Completing a **Tier Challenge** given by the Dungeon.

Tier Challenges test Rhythm mastery:

- **Tier 1:** Show you understand Balance.
- **Tier 2:** Show you can wield Heat.
- **Tier 3:** Show you control Momentum.
- **Tier 4:** Show the Dungeon that you belong here.
- **Tier 5:** The Dungeon asks what you really want.

```
[DUNGEON NOTICE]
‚ÄúA test reveals truth. You cannot lie with your rhythm.‚Äù
```

# ‚≠ê **11.4 ENTERTAINMENT RATING ‚Äî THE DUNGEON WATCHES YOU**

Every encounter modifies your **Entertainment Rating**, a hidden value the Dungeon tracks.

High Entertainment Rating grants:

- Bonus Veinscore
- Free Resolve
- Achievements
- Sudden gifts (Relics, Resolve Actions)
- Favorable UI anomalies (slow motion, hints)

Low Entertainment Rating triggers:

- Harder enemies
- More Interrupt attempts
- Less Resolve gain
- Dungeon mockery

### **Entertainment Rating rises when you:**

- Use Perfect Defense multiple times
- Run long, stylish chains
- Use Rhythm smartly (Balance ‚Üí Heat ‚Üí Momentum ‚Üí Heat Burst)
- Execute dramatically
- Take risks while low HP

### **Entertainment Rating drops when you:**

- Play safe repeatedly
- Repeat the same action patterns
- Ignore Heat
- Never Parry or Dodge
- Fail to impress

```
[DUNGEON DELIGHT]
‚ÄúDance for me. Or bleed for me. Either is fine.‚Äù
```
