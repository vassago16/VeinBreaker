# ü©∏ **SECTION 2 ‚Äî CORE SYSTEMS OVERVIEW (Dynamic Rhythm Model)**

Veinbreaker‚Äôs combat is built on three interlocking flows:

## **1. BALANCE ‚Äî Your Offensive Rhythm**

Balance measures how stable, centered, or overextended you are during a chain. It changes with each declared action.

### **Balance States**

- **Positive Balance (+1 to +3)**: Perfect form, precise footwork, tactical control.\
  *Gained from Perfect Defense or Balance Surge.*
- **Neutral (0)**: Centered stance; no bonuses or penalties.
- **Negative (‚Äì1 to ‚Äì10)**: Overextension from attacks, abilities, and heavy actions.

### **Effects of Balance**

Balance modifies your **Attack Value (AV)**:

- Positive Balance **adds** to Attack Value.
- Negative Balance **subtracts** from Attack Value.

Balance resets to **0 at the start of your turn** unless a debuff prevents it.

```
[DUNGEON NOTICE: Balance Shift Detected]
‚ÄúYour rhythm changes‚Ä¶ I smell intent.‚Äù
```

---

## **2. HEAT ‚Äî Your Aggressive Flow**

Heat rises when attacks hit.\
Heat resets when your chain ends.

### **Heat Thresholds**

- **Heat 1:** No bonus
- **Heat 2:** +1 damage
- **Heat 3:** +2 damage
- **Heat 4:** +3 damage, increased Execution pressure
- **Heat 5:** +4 damage, high Interrupt risk

### **Heat Spend (New Core Mechanic)**

Heat can be spent **between actions in a chain**.

**Heat Burst:** Spend all Heat ‚Üí add **Heat √ó 2** bonus damage.\
**Heat Purge:** Spend all Heat ‚Üí Balance resets to **0** (chain continues).\
**Heat Stabilize:** At Heat 3+ ‚Üí preserve up to **2 Heat** into next turn.

```
[DUNGEON THRILL: Heat Rising]
‚ÄúPush harder. I want to see you burn.‚Äù
```

---

## **3. MOMENTUM ‚Äî Your Defensive Rhythm**

Momentum rises when you successfully Dodge, Parry, or Block. It fuels **Perfect Defense**, allowing:

- Perfect Dodge
- Perfect Parry
- Perfect Block

**Perfect Defense ‚Üí +1 Positive Balance**

Momentum resets at the start of your turn.

```
[DUNGEON APPROVAL]
‚ÄúYou move well when survival matters.‚Äù
```

---

## **4. RESOLVE ‚Äî Your Action Currency**

Resolve (RP) is spent to declare actions in a chain. Every action costs **1 RP**, minimum.

You gain RP when you:

- Hit an enemy
- Perfect Defend
- Execute
- Impress the Dungeon (rare bonuses)

Resolve refreshes **2 RP at the start of your turn**, up to your Resolve cap (Tier 0 cap: **5**). Unspent RP carries over between turns; you cannot exceed your cap.

---

## **5. ATTACK VALUE (AV) & DEFENSE VALUE (DV)**

Veinbreaker uses the **A3 Contest System**:

### **ATTACK VALUE = d20 + Bonuses ‚Äì Balance Penalty + Positive Balance**

### **DEFENSE VALUE = d20 + Momentum + IDF + situational modifiers**

Attack Value vs Defense Value determines hit or miss.

Margin ‚â• 5 ‚Üí **Perfect Defense or Perfect Block** triggers special effects.

## **6.7 INTERRUPTS ‚Äî WHEN THE DUNGEON PUNISHES GREED**

### **Interrupt Contest**


If enemy wins by:

- **0‚Äì4** ‚Üí Chain continues (you take hit)
- **5+** ‚Üí **Chain breaks immediately**

- You are Interrupted
- You are stunned

## **7.1 What Is a Reaction?**

A **Reaction** is a defensive technique that may be used when you are directly threatened.

A Reaction:

- Does **not** consume a chain action slot
- May only be used if the specific Reaction is **not on cooldown**
- Requires you to have the proper **weapon or conduit tag**

# ‚≠ê **12.1 What Is Execution?**

Execution is a special finishing action a player may **declare** when‚Äîand only when‚Äîthe target is **Primed**.

Execution:

- Costs **1 RP**
- Consumes **your next declared action slot**, replacing its attack
- Uses a special A3 Contest with higher risk and higher stakes
- On success ‚Üí kills or catastrophically wounds the target
- On failure ‚Üí exposes you to immediate counter‚Äëpressure

Execution is **never automatic**. Even at 0 HP, the Dungeon requires the moment to *mean* something.

# ‚≠ê **12.2 Primed ‚Äî The Only Execution Trigger**

- The target is at **‚â§ 25% HP**

| Attacker Position This Round | Boss DV Modifier |
| ---------------------------- | ---------------- |

- Prevents "4 players Nova at once" trivialization
- Makes earlier attacks safer but less damaging
- Makes late‚Äëround attacks riskier but potentially Heat‚Äëboosted
