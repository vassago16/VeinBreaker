# Equipment & Tags (Quick Guide)

Weapons and focuses don’t raise damage dice—they grant permissions and hooks for abilities and reactions. Pick tags that match how you want to fight.

## Tag List (Core)
- **Slashing:** Can apply Bleed; often pairs with Parry/Riposte actions.
- **Blunt:** Can apply Stagger or Stun; pairs with Block/Heavy Guard.
- **Piercing:** Can apply Pierce/Armor-ignoring effects; precision strikes.
- **Finesse:** Enables agile reactions like Parry/Riposte/Slip.
- **Heavy:** Enables Block/Guard Break/Heavy Attack lines; suits forceful styles.
- **Shield:** Enables Block/Perfect Block/Stability actions.
- **Magic Conduit:** Channels arcane power; enables Redirect/Deflect and spell actions.
- **Faith Conduit:** Channels radiant/spiritual power; enables Ward/Radiance and miracle actions.

## How Tags Work
- Tags are permission slips: they tell you what actions and status riders you’re allowed to use (e.g., Bleed from Slashing, Stagger from Blunt, Ward from Faith Conduit).
- Abilities reference tags to check compatibility. If you lack the tag, you can’t use that tagged action or effect.
- Tags do **not** change your damage die; actions and abilities set the damage/effects.

## Starting Equipment (Tier 0 Examples)
- Martial: Axe (Slashing, Heavy), Warhammer (Blunt, Heavy), Spear (Piercing), Shield (Shield).
- Shadow: Dagger (Slashing, Finesse), Stiletto (Piercing, Finesse).
- Magic: Arcane Focus (Magic Conduit), Channel Rod (Magic Conduit), Spell Thread (Magic Conduit, Finesse).
- Faith: Holy Brand (Faith Conduit), Prayer Vessel (Faith Conduit, Heavy), Ritual Chain (Faith Conduit, Slashing).

Pick the tags that unlock the reactions/abilities you want; swap flavor freely as long as the tags stay consistent.
