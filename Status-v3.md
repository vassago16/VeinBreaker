A unified reference for all major harmful conditions and debuffs.

| **Debuff**     | **Effect Description**                                                                                  | **Stacks / Scaling**                                                          | **Additional Notes**                                           |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Vulnerable** | Target’s defenses are compromised; attacks or abilities that interact with Vulnerable gain full effect. | At **3 stacks**, the enemy becomes **Primed** for execution‑enhanced effects. | Common trigger for Shadow & Martial synergy abilities.         |
| **Primed**     | The enemy is fully exposed—certain abilities deal bonus damage or gain special triggers.                | Binary (on/off).                                                              | Often triggered by Vulnerable 3 or by heavy stance breaks.     |
| **Telegraph**  | The attacker telegraphs a big move. Defender gains **+1 to Defense rolls during Interrupt challenges**. | Does not stack.                                                               | Usually applied when an enemy prepares a heavy or slow attack. |
| **Bleed**      | Target takes recurring damage each round or per hit.                                                    | Stackable; damage increases with stacks.                                      | Many Shadow abilities add Bleed.                               |
| **Stagger**    | Target’s posture is disrupted; they lose effectiveness on next offensive or defensive roll.             | Typically 1–2 stacks; high stacks can remove actions.                         | Often inflicted by Martial heavy attacks.                      |
| **Slowed**     | Target suffers reduced movement capability; penalties to movement or chase actions.                     | Does not stack; refreshed by new application.                                 | Magic frost/ice effects frequently apply this.                 |
| **Distracted** | Target suffers a penalty (usually –1) to their next attack roll.                                        | Does not stack.                                                               | Often caused by magical deflection or mirror effects.          |
| **Exhausted**  | Character has **0 RP** at the start of their turn; cannot declare a chain or take actions.              | Clears when RP > 0.                                                           | Utility or Faith abilities can remove it.                      |
