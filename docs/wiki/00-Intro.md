WHAT IS VEINBREAKER?**

> *“Before you read the rules, understand this: the Dungeon is already reading you.”*

You were not chosen. You were **taken**.

One moment you lived in the world you knew—screens, streetlights, heartbeat mundane.\
The next, you were somewhere else.

Stone that **breathes like muscle**. Doors that **shrink like pupils**.\
A whisper in the dark that said your name—correctly.

**Welcome, Veinbreaker.**

The Dungeon saw your life.\
Your fears.\
Your regrets.\
And it *smiled*.

This place is alive.\
It watches you.\
It learns you.\
It wants to see what you’ll do when cornered—\
when blood hits stone—\
when **Resolve burns hotter than fear**.

You do not play a survivor.\
You play a fighter who **weaponizes knowledge**.\
You break patterns.\
You break chains.\
You break the Dungeon before it breaks you.

#  **THE RULES HERE ARE MADE TO BE BROKEN**

Veinbreaker is a high‑risk, momentum‑driven action RPG with three interconnected combat currents:

### **1. Balance — Your Rhythm**

Balance measures how steady or off‑center you are during a Chain.\
It is shaped by technique, footwork, foresight, and chaos.

### **2. Heat — Your Fury**

Heat rises with every successful strike.\
It builds power. It tempts greed.\
It makes the Dungeon’s heart beat faster.

### **3. Momentum — Your Defense Rhythm**

Momentum grows when you deflect, dodge, or defend with precision. It allows Perfect Defense and fuels rhythm mastery.

Together, Balance + Heat + Momentum form **Dynamic Rhythm Combat**. This system rewards mastery, intentionality, and style.

#  **THE DUNGEON KNOWS YOU ARE PLAYING**

The Dungeon remembers Earth—your UI logic, your instinct for progress bars, pop‑ups, and stat sheets.\
It builds a language you will understand.

Your **character sheet exists inside your mind**. Your **inventory** is a spatial anomaly—anything you hold for 10 seconds becomes yours. Your **status effects** appear as luminous glyphs in your peripheral vision. Your **notifications** whisper.

```
[DUNGEON NOTICE: Momentum Rising]
“You learn quickly. Good.”
```

Meta isn’t cheating.\
Meta is survival.\
Meta is power.

But power has teeth. Every chain tempts.\
Every victory stains.\
**Blood Marks whisper your name louder each time.**

Live long enough, and the Dungeon will notice.\
Live longer, and the Dungeon may want you.
