## **4.2 Step 2 ‚Äî Determine Derived Stats**

Derived stats calculate your survivability and combat readiness.

### **HP (Health Points)**

```
HP = 10 + POWER
```

### **Pools**

```
Martial Pool = POWER √∑ 2
Shadow Pool  = AGILITY √∑ 2
Magic Pool   = MIND √∑ 2
Faith Pool   = SPIRIT √∑ 2
```

(Rounded down)

Pools regenerate each round.

### **IDF (Interrupt Defense)**

Your resistance to being Interrupted.

IDF comes from:

- Momentum
- Buffs (Defense Up, Stances)
- Equipment (shields, wards)
- Abilities

Base IDF = **0** unless modified.

Used in:

```
DEFENSE VALUE = d20 + Momentum + IDF + situational modifiers
```

### **Resolve (RP)**

Resolve is your **tempo resource**, spent to declare actions in a chain.

### **Resolve Now Uses the Hybrid System**

- **Max Resolve = 5** at character creation
- **Resolve refreshes 2 RP at the start of your turn**, up to your Max Resolve
- You may carry unused Resolve from previous turns
- Resolve cannot exceed your **Max Resolve** (5 at Tier 0, increases with higher Tiers)

### **Sources of Bonus Resolve**

- Hitting an enemy: **+1 RP**
- Perfect Defense: **+1 RP**
- Execution: **+1 or more RP**
- Dungeon Favor: variable

### **Resolve Example**

Turn 1: You start with 5 RP, spend 4, end turn with 1 RP.
Turn 2: Regenerate 2 RP, start with 3 RP.
Turn 3: Regenerate 2 RP, start with 5 RP (cap).

## **4.4 Step 4 ‚Äî Choose Your Tier 0 Starting Abilities**

Every new Veinbreaker chooses **four Tier‚Äë0 abilities**:

- **1 Attack**
- **1 Reaction** (Dodge, Parry, Block, Slip, Ward, etc.)
- **1 Utility** (Bless, Rhythm prep, minor support)
- **1 Movement Action** (Dash, Shift, Withdraw, Advance ‚Äî coming in the Movement Action expansion)

Tier 0 abilities cost **0 Pool** but **1 RP** to declare as part of a chain.

You begin with:

- A simple attack
- A defensive maneuver
- A utility or supportive action
- A movement option to shape Balance and positioning

Tier 0 provides your **opening rhythm**, now including your first mobility tool ‚Äî essential for Balance control and chain shaping.\*\*.
