## SECTION 7 ‚Äî REACTIONS (Dynamic Defense & Perfect Flow) (Dynamic Defense & Perfect Flow)\*\*

Reactions are **instant defensive techniques** used when an enemy targets you with an attack, spell, or threat. They do **not** cost an action slot in your declared chain, but they **do** require that you have an applicable Reaction ability that is **not on cooldown**.

Reactions shape **Momentum**, enable **Perfect Defense**, and determine whether you hold your rhythm‚Äîor lose it.

## **7.1 What Is a Reaction?**

A **Reaction** is a defensive technique that may be used when you are directly threatened.

A Reaction:

- Costs **1 RP**
- Uses an **A3 Contest** (your DV vs enemy AV)
- Does **not** consume a chain action slot
- May only be used if the specific Reaction is **not on cooldown**
- Requires you to have the proper **weapon or conduit tag**
- Can only be used **once per incoming threat**

Reactions are the player‚Äôs real‚Äëtime adjustments within the Dungeon‚Äôs rhythm.

---

## **7.2 Reaction Windows**

You may React only inside specific timing windows.

### **Attack Window**

When you are targeted by a melee or ranged attack.

### **Spell Window**

When a magical attack or harmful arcane effect targets you.

### **Threat Window**

During an enemy **Interrupt** attempt.

### **Movement Window** *(rare)*

Some advanced techniques or magical manipulations may allow Reactions when an enemy moves.

Only **one Reaction** may be used per threat.

## **7.5 Momentum & Reactions**

Momentum rises through successful defensive rhythm.

- Dodge ‚Üí +1 Momentum
- Parry ‚Üí +1 Momentum
- Block ‚Üí +1 Momentum
- Ward/Redirect ‚Üí +1 Momentum

Momentum increases your **Defense Value**:

```
DEFENSE VALUE = d20 + Momentum + IDF + bonuses
```

Momentum resets at the start of your turn.

Positive Momentum is essential for contesting Interrupts.

## **7.8 Resolve Actions & Reactions (Interaction Rules)**

Resolve Actions may be used **during Reaction Windows**, but they do **not** replace Reactions.

Examples:

- **Rhythm Break** can cancel an Interrupt after a failed Parry.
- **Momentum Anchor** can raise DV before a Block roll.
- **Disengage** can end your chain before the Block resolves.
- **Balance Surge** can stabilize Balance after a Perfect Defense.

Resolve Actions modify the **flow around** Reactions‚Äînot the Reactions themselves.
