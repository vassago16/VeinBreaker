## **6.3 THE FIRST ACTION ‚Äî THE A3 CONTEST BEGINS**

The first action in your chain establishes your **initial Attack Value (AV)**.

### ‚úî Balance does **not** affect the first action.

Opening attack is always neutral unless modified by buffs/debuffs.

### **Attack Value Formula (Simplified)**

```

- **Balance Modifier** = +X (positive), ‚ÄìX (negative), or 0
- **Offensive Modifiers** come from abilities, Heat, weapon tags, position

### **Defense Value (DV)**

```

## **6.8 ENDING THE CHAIN**

A chain ends when:

- You complete all declared actions

### When the chain ends:
