# Glossary

Quick definitions for common Veinbreaker terms and abbreviations.

- **A3 Contest System**: The opposed roll structure; AV vs DV each action.
- **AV (Attack Value)**: d20 + bonuses ± Balance; the attacker’s contest roll.
- **DV (Defense Value)**: d20 + Momentum + IDF + situational modifiers; the defender’s contest roll.
- **Balance**: Accuracy modifier; positive adds to AV, negative subtracts. Resets to 0 at your turn start unless modified.
- **Heat**: Damage ramp that builds on hits; thresholds add damage; can be Burst/Purged/Stabilized.
- **Momentum**: Defensive rhythm gained on successful defenses; fuels Perfect Defense; resets at your turn start.
- **RP (Resolve Points)**: Action currency; pay 1 RP per action in a chain. Refresh 2 per turn up to cap; carry over to cap.
- **Pools**: Path resource pools (Martial/Shadow/Magic/Faith), typically attribute/2, refreshed each round.
- **IDF (Interrupt Defense)**: Flat defensive modifier used in DV; from gear, stances, buffs, etc.
- **Chain**: Declared sequence of actions on your turn; RP paid upfront; can end early if interrupted by damage.
- **Perfect Defense/Block**: DV margin (typically 5+) that grants benefits (e.g., +1 Balance, riposte/retaliate windows).
- **Stabilize Heat**: Option to carry Heat into the next turn (usually 2 at Heat 3+).
- **Burst/Purge**: Spend all Heat for bonus damage (Burst) or to reset Balance while continuing your chain (Purge).
- **Execution**: Finisher on Primed targets; replaces next action, heavy Balance penalty, resets Heat.
- **Off-Balance**: Status that makes you start next turn at a negative Balance (e.g., −2).
- **Primed**: Target meets execution conditions (e.g., Vulnerable stacks, low HP) allowing an Execution.
- **Blood Marks**: Persistent marks gained on Executes; grant power and draw Dungeon attention.
