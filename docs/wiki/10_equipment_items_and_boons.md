# ‚≠ê **10.1 Equipment Philosophy ‚Äî Less Math, More Rhythm**

Unlike traditional RPGs, Veinbreaker‚Äôs equipment avoids stat bloat.

Equipment affects:

- What **actions** you can perform (Parry, Block, Redirect‚Ä¶)
- How you interact with **Balance, Heat, and Momentum**
- What **status effects** you can inflict
- What **Reactions** you are allowed to use
- What **Resolve Actions** you can unlock or enhance

Equipment does *not* affect:

- Accuracy (A3 Contest handles that)
- Base damage (abilities handle that)
- Armor Class (does not exist)

Equipment shapes **style**, not math.

```
[DUNGEON NOTE]
‚ÄúA tool is interesting only when it tempts you to use it poorly.‚Äù
```

# ‚≠ê **10.2 Weapon Categories & Tags**

| -------------------- | ---------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| **Light Weapons**    | dagger, shortblade, stiletto | Slashing, Piercing, Finesse |

Weapons do **not** dictate damage‚Äîthey dictate *moments*.

# ‚≠ê **10.3 Armor & Defensive Gear**

Armor does not give AC. Instead, it interacts with Rhythm.

### **Light Armor**

- +1 to AGI‚Äëbased Movement contests
- Reduces Balance penalties from Movement by 1 (min 0)

### **Medium Armor**

- +1 to resisting **Stagger**
- +1 IDF vs Heavy Attacks

### **Heavy Armor**

- Stacks with Shields for **+1 Perfect Block Margin**
- +1 to resisting Bleed, but Movement has Disadvantage

Armor is always a tradeoff.

# ‚≠ê **10.9 Equipment & Rhythm ‚Äî Unified Flow**

Equipment should always:

- Present new tactical questions
- Open or close Rhythm options
- Change Reaction availability
- Modify how Heat, Balance, or Momentum behave

Equipment should **never**:

- Inflate numbers mindlessly
- Replace skill expression
- Overshadow Path abilities

Your weapon says *how* you fight.\
Your Path says *why*.\
Your Rhythm says *whether you live*.
